VEDAS

The Vedas are the religious texts which inform the religion of Hinduism,
.The term veda means “knowledge” in that they are thought to contain the
fundamental knowledge relating to the underlying cause of, function of, and
personal response to existence.

ANCIENT EDUCATION SYSTEM OF INDIA

There are inscriptions on stones and copper, palm leaf records and our
scriptures as evidences of the historic origins of learning in India
The system focused on the moral, physical, spiritual and intellectual
aspects of life

VIKRAMSHILA UNIVERSITY

Vikramashila was one of the two most important centres of learning in
India during the Pala Empire, along with Nalanda. Its location is now
the site of Antichak village, Bhagalpur district in Bihar
